Phase 2: Resource Governance — Optimized and Fair Allocation

Value: ROI and fairness. Right-sized, shared resources instead of dedicated hoarding.

Outcome

Associates understand how to expose compute (including GPUs) as a governed menu and increase utilization via sharing.

Takeaway

  • Hardware Profiles hide Kubernetes complexity; GPU as a Service (time-slicing, MIG, Kueue) maximizes utilization.

  • Engineering outcome: Hardware profiles and GPU sharing configured for cost and fairness.
