Phase 5: Quality and SLOs — Model Governance and Business Validation

Value: Trust and fit. Deployment is not enough; validation proves fit-for-purpose and supports SLOs.

Outcome

Associates can define validation criteria from business requirements and run or automate validation (accuracy, performance, domain).

Takeaway

  • Business value of validation; application-specific validation (multi-language, performance, domain); optional automated validation services (Ch3 marked Advanced/Experimental).

  • Engineering outcome: Validation integrated into the deployment and operations workflow.

Use the navigation menu to work through the Validation modules (Introduction, Business Requirements, ROI; Application-Specific Validation; Deploying Validation Services).

Checkpoint

  • Validation job run and report produced; criteria tied to business requirements.
