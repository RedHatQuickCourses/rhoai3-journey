Results Analysis & Business Insights

Validation results are data. Analysis turns data into business decisions.

This module teaches you to analyze validation results across all dimensions (accuracy, performance, domain) and extract actionable business insights.

Analyzing Multi-Dimensional Results

Combine results from all validation dimensions:

Accuracy Analysis

  • Compare accuracy across languages

  • Identify language-specific gaps

  • Assess overall model quality

Performance Analysis

  • Evaluate latency against business requirements

  • Assess throughput for scalability

  • Identify performance bottlenecks

Domain Analysis

  • Measure domain-specific understanding

  • Identify areas needing improvement

  • Assess business readiness

Creating Business Reports

Generate stakeholder-ready reports that answer:

  • Is the model ready for production? (Overall assessment)

  • What are the risks? (Identified gaps and limitations)

  • What are the recommendations? (Next steps and improvements)
