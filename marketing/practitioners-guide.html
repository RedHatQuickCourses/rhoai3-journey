Your Learning Journey: Four Strategic Phases

Phase 0: Operator Ecosystem

What You Build: Install and configure the 7+ operators required for RHOAI 3.x (NFD, NVIDIA GPU, cert-manager, Kueue, etc.)

What You Learn: How the OpenShift operator ecosystem provides the foundation for multi-tenant, GPU-enabled AI platforms

Time Investment: 2-4 hours | Skills Gained: Operator installation, cluster policy configuration, GPU operator management

Phase 1: Assembling the Factory (Configure)

What You Build: Model registry backed by MySQL/Postgres, governed model catalog, hardware profiles, GPU-as-a-Service with time-slicing and MIG

What You Learn: Why you start with model requirements before infrastructure sizing, how to eliminate Shadow AI through governance, strategies for 3-5x GPU utilization improvement

Time Investment: 8-12 hours | Skills Gained: Model governance, infrastructure abstraction, resource scheduling, GPU sharing strategies

Phase 2: Token Production Engine (Serve)

What You Build: High-throughput model serving with vLLM and KServe, optimized inference configurations, cost-reduction through model compression

What You Learn: VRAM sizing mathematics, vLLM tuning parameters, when compression makes sense vs. when it doesn't, production deployment patterns

Time Investment: 6-10 hours | Skills Gained: Inference optimization, KServe architecture, production tuning, performance benchmarking

Phase 3: Scaling the Utility (Scale)

What You Build: Distributed inference with llm-d, internal Model-as-a-Service (MaaS) platform with API gateway, observability with Prometheus/Grafana

What You Learn: When to use distributed inference vs. single-node serving, how to expose AI as a product to internal customers, cost attribution and chargeback models

Time Investment: 6-10 hours | Skills Gained: Distributed systems, API gateway configuration, multi-tenancy, observability

Phase 4: Governance & Compound Systems (Validate)

What You Build: Trust frameworks with TrustyAI, input/output guardrails, foundation for autonomous agents and compound AI systems

What You Learn: Zero-trust architecture for AI, compliance validation, safe deployment of agentic workflows

Time Investment: 4-8 hours | Skills Gained: AI safety, compliance frameworks, advanced AI architectures
