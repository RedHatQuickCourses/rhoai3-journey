## INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the Red Hat OpenShift AI 3.x Foundations course series—a unified learning journey designed to help your organization move from a greenfield OpenShift cluster to an operational, production-grade AI platform in 10 weeks.

**A Note on This Series:**
These courses were created with AI assistance. They provide basic, functional code to stand up your environment quickly. This code is **not production-ready CI/CD**—that's your next phase. Our focus here is technical implementation driven by business decisions and real problems you're solving. We show you *how to get a result* so you can explore the platform as a whole. You'll learn deeper troubleshooting and architectural complexity later, after you have a working environment.

---

## THE CHALLENGE & SOLUTION

**The Problem:**
- How fast can you move from "we want AI" to "AI delivers business value"?
- Most organizations struggle with 6–18 months of infrastructure setup before development begins.
- GPU costs are high; governance is hard; complexity is distributed.

**Our Approach:**
A progressive, opinionated architecture that's a starting point—not the final destination. We show you the business decision, then the technical implementation. No fluff. Result-focused.

---

## THE LEARNING JOURNEY: 5 PHASES

1. **Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2):** Storage, Registry, Model Catalog
2. **Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4):** Hardware Profiles, GPU as a Service
3. **Phase 3 (Weeks 5-6):** Platform Assembly & Integration
4. **Phase 4 (Weeks 7-9):** LLM Deployment, Models as a Service (MaaS)
5. **Phase 5 (Week 10):** Validation, Observability, Operations

Each module follows: **Business Value → Architecture → Implementation → Validation**

---

## WHY THIS WORKS

- **Progressive:** Each phase builds on the last; understand *why* before executing *how*
- **Hands-On:** Copy-paste ready YAML and scripts; deployed on day one
- **Business-Aligned:** Technical decisions tied to business outcomes
- **Extensible:** Opinionated starting point; adapt and extend for production

---

## YOUR PATH FORWARD

1. **Learn:** Complete rhoai3-journey in 10 weeks (20–30 hours total)
2. **Operate:** Run your AI platform; measure business impact
3. **Extend:** Create custom modules for your specific domain, compliance, or workload
4. **Contribute:** Share your modules with the community

---

## CALL TO ACTION

Start with Phase 1 this week. Get storage, registry, and catalog running. Explore the platform. Once you have the foundation, everything else becomes easier—and you'll be ready to invest in production-grade tooling and governance.

The real learning happens when you run it yourself.
